Hi, I'm Shane!

.

I believe place matters. Where we are and where we come from define our sense of the world. Maps are the medium to display place, and an elegant and effective map can convey complex and nuanced details in a way that no other medium can. More importantly, maps do it at a personal level. All a person needs to see is their place in the story!


I currently live on a small island in the San Francisco Bay and often think about my place in my community and the challenges we are going to face in the future. I am grateful to have spent the last few years in an industry where making sense of place and creating innovate spatial solutions to these challenges is at its core. I have almost fours years of work experience in Geography, GIS, and cartography and am an aspiring Web Map Applications Developer.
